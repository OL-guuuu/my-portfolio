# PRODUCT VISION

## What This Is

A **cinematic editorial portfolio experience** that immerses visitors in a designer's creative workspace.

This is not a resume site. This is not a gallery. This is a **designed experience** that demonstrates design thinking through its own existence.

---

## Purpose

### For Visitors
- Experience the designer's world firsthand
- Understand the designer's approach through spatial storytelling
- Engage with work in a memorable, premium context
- Feel the quality and intentionality of the designer's craft

### For the Designer
- Showcase work in a unique, brand-aligned environment
- Demonstrate technical and aesthetic capabilities
- Stand out from conventional portfolio sites
- Control the narrative and presentation

---

## Core Philosophy

### Experience Over Information
The portfolio prioritizes **experiential understanding** over information density.

Visitors should **feel** the designer's approach before they read about it.

### Restraint as Excellence
By choosing **not** to do certain things, we elevate what remains.

- No flashy effects → Intentional motion has impact
- No bright colors → Grayscale becomes sophisticated
- No clutter → Each element commands attention

### Object-Centered Design
The workspace objects are not decoration—they are **narrative devices**.

Each object tells part of the story:
- The monitor shows the work
- The desk grounds the designer
- The tools reveal the process
- The space conveys the mindset

## Design Principles

### 1. Coherence Above All
Every element must belong to the singular workspace world.

### 2. Quality Over Quantity
One premium object beats ten mediocre ones.

### 3. Narrative First
If it doesn't serve the story, it doesn't belong.

### 4. Calm Confidence
No need to shout—the work speaks for itself.

### 5. User Respect
Never trick, trap, or annoy the visitor.

## Creative Reference & Inspiration

### Lusion (https://lusion.co/)

**Reference For:**
- **Quality bar**: The level of premium execution we aspire to
- **Atmosphere**: How mood and depth create immersive experiences
- **Restraint**: Less is more, intentionality over abundance
- **Cinematic pacing**: Thoughtful, deliberate storytelling
- **Premium digital experience**: High-end editorial quality

**NOT a Reference For:**
- Direct copying or cloning
- Layout structures to replicate
- Specific interactions to mimic
- Components or sections to duplicate
- 3D/WebGL techniques to copy
- Visual effects to reproduce
- Changing our designer workspace world

**How to Use This Reference:**
1. Study Lusion's **quality standards** and **attention to detail**
2. Understand their **restraint** and **intentional choices**
3. Feel the **mood** and **cinematic pacing** they create
4. Apply those **principles** to our **unique designer workspace world**
5. Never copy directly - always create **original work** rooted in our concept

**Our World Remains:**
A singular designer's office / creative workspace. Monochrome. Premium. Restrained. Editorial.
